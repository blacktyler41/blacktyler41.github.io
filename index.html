About Us

Who we are and what we stand for

St John Minor Seminary Nkumba provides holistic education, helping students grow spiritually, intellectually, and morally. Our mission is to prepare young men for priesthood and Leadership in the Church and society.

  • Strong spiritual formation
  • High academic standards
  • Discipline and character development
